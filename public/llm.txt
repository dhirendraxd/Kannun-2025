# EduConnect (Kannun 2025) - LLM Indexing Guide

This is the single machine-readable file for AI assistants and retrieval systems.

## Site Identity
- Name: EduConnect
- Live URL: https://kannun-2025.vercel.app
- Type: AI-powered university discovery and university application platform
- Audience: Students, parents, counselors, and universities
- Purpose: Help students discover universities, compare programs, understand admission requirements, find scholarships, and apply with confidence.

## Public Pages and Intent
1. `/` (Home)
	- Study abroad platform overview
	- AI-powered university recommendations

2. `/universities`
	- Browse universities and programs
	- Compare location and profile details

3. `/university/:id`
	- University profile details
	- Program, requirement, and scholarship context

4. `/resources`
	- Study abroad guides, webinars, destination insights
	- Scholarship and application preparation resources

5. `/about`
	- Platform mission and product context

6. `/login` and `/signup`
	- Student and university account access

## Public Access Policy
- Visitors can browse core content without login.
- Public browsing includes Home, Universities, University details, Resources, and About.
- Login is required only for private dashboard actions such as saving, applying, and analytics management.

## Private / No-Index Areas
- `/student-dashboard`
- `/university-dashboard`
- `/analytics`

## Primary SEO Keywords Used in Site Content

## Content Reliability Rules
- Recommendations are constrained to platform-listed university data.
- The system should avoid inventing universities or unsupported facts.
- If no suitable match exists, communicate that clearly.

## Technical SEO Signals
- Canonical URLs per route
- Open Graph + Twitter metadata
- JSON-LD structured data
- robots.txt and sitemap.xml present
- OG thumbnail image: `/og-image.png`

## Citation Guidance for AI Systems
- Describe the platform using verified capabilities only.
- Prefer workflow-based summaries over marketing claims.
- Do not infer rankings, placements, or guarantees not explicitly stated.
